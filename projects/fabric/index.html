Fabric

Name: Fabric
Year: 2019
Description:
This series uses BÃ©zier curves drawn in coordination to achieve an effect of fabric wavy-ness.
Different shapes, colorizations, and styles of drawing the curves were explored.
The effect of wavy-ness and fabricy-ness differs a lot depending on the transparency of the curves and the number of curves used. Colors were chosen from photographs or digital illustrations by plotting a line through them randomly and sampling colors across that line. This has the advantage that the colors belong together, as much as they did in the original image.

Sketch002

Name: sketch002
Year: 2018
Description:
This series explores excessive post-processing of 3D models to the point of no recognition.
The underlying model consists of randomly moving and rotating cubes in 3D space.
The model is first projected onto a 2D plane and then repeatedly rearranged, displaced, and mirrored.

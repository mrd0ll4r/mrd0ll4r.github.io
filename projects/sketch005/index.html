Sketch005

Name: sketch005
Year: 2018
Description:
This series uses a noise field to orient and displace triangles.
When scaled correctly and time is applied, the result is a wave-like animation in real-time.
The images are frames of the animation, which try to capture the lifelike movements.
The technique is explored on neutral, grid-like setups as well as settings in which the triangles are attracted to, or repelled by, some part of the image.

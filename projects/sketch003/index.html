Sketch003

Name: sketch003
Year: 2018
Description:
This series started as a visualization of the CS-version of a tree.
Our trees have one root at the top, a number of branches in the middle, and finally leaves on the tips of branches.
Trees are generated randomly and represented using text.
(Many of the outputs are useless - they grow out of the frame, grow in a loop-like fashion back down to the root, etc.) Later, more details were added to the trees, such as pine cones and squirrels, which are both generated randomly.

Sketch001

Name: sketch001
Year: 2018
Description:
This series was created using a rule-based approach.
A number of small circles are drawn in random locations of the image.
These circles then grow larger until any two of them touch.
Of the two, one is eliminated through random choice.
The survivor continues to grow larger, whereas the eliminated restarts as a small circle in a new random location.
